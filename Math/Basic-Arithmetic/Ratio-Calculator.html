About Ratios

A ratio compares two or more quantities. It shows how many times one number contains another. Ratios can be simplified just like fractions.

Aspect Ratios

Aspect ratio describes the proportional relationship between width and height of an image or screen. Common ratios include 16:9 for TVs and 4:3 for older monitors.

Quick Tips

  • Use colons (:) to separate terms.
  • Ratios can have more than 2 terms.
  • Aspect ratio calculation helps in resizing images.
