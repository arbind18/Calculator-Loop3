About Decimals

A decimal is a way of writing a number that is not whole. It uses a decimal point to separate the whole number part from the fractional part.

Conversions

Fraction to Decimal: Divide the numerator by the denominator.
Decimal to Fraction: Write the decimal as a fraction with a denominator of 10, 100, 1000, etc., and simplify.
Base Conversion: Decimals (Base 10) can be converted to Binary (Base 2), Octal (Base 8), and Hexadecimal (Base 16) for computing applications.

Quick Tips
