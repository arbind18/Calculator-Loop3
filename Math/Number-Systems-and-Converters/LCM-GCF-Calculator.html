Definitions

GCF (Greatest Common Factor): Also known as GCD (Greatest Common Divisor), it is the largest positive integer that divides each of the integers without a remainder.

LCM (Least Common Multiple): The smallest positive integer that is divisible by each of the numbers.

Relationship: For two numbers $a$ and $b$: $LCM(a, b) \times GCF(a, b) = |a \times b|$.
