How It Works

2x2 Matrix

For a matrix $A = \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}$, the determinant is:

$$ |A| = ad - bc $$

3x3 Matrix

For a matrix $A = \begin{pmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{pmatrix}$, the determinant is calculated using expansion by minors:

$$ |A| = a(ei - fh) - b(di - fg) + c(dh - eg) $$
