Predict Y for a given X

Formulas

$$m = \frac{n(\sum xy) - (\sum x)(\sum y)}{n(\sum x^2) - (\sum x)^2}$$ $$b = \frac{\sum y - m(\sum x)}{n}$$ $$r = \frac{n(\sum xy) - (\sum x)(\sum y)}{\sqrt{[n\sum x^2 - (\sum x)^2][n\sum y^2 - (\sum y)^2]}}$$

Where:

$n$ = Number of data points

$\sum xy$ = Sum of the product of X and Y

$\sum x$ = Sum of X values

$\sum y$ = Sum of Y values

$\sum x^2$ = Sum of squared X values
