Confidence Interval Calculator

Estimate the range of a population parameter

-
Margin of Error: -

Formula

$$CI = \bar{x} \pm Z \left( \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}} \right)$$

Where:

$\bar{x}$ = Sample Mean

$Z$ = Z-score corresponding to the confidence level

$s$ = Sample Standard Deviation

$n$ = Sample Size
