Formulas

Law of Sines:

$$\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C}$$

To find a side (b):

$$b = \frac{a \cdot \sin B}{\sin A}$$

To find an angle (B):

$$\sin B = \frac{b \cdot \sin A}{a}$$

Note: Angles are in degrees.
