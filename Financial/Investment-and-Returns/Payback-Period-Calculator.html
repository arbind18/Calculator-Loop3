About Payback Period Calculator

The Payback Period Calculator helps you determine how long it will take to recover the cost of an investment. It is a simple and widely used method for evaluating the risk and liquidity of a project.

What is Payback Period?
The payback period is the length of time required to recover the cost of an investment. It is calculated by dividing the initial investment by the annual cash inflow.
Why is a shorter payback period better?
A shorter payback period is generally preferred because it means the investor recovers their capital sooner, reducing the risk of loss from long-term uncertainties. It also improves liquidity.
Does this calculator consider the time value of money?
No, this is a simple payback period calculator. It does not account for the time value of money (discounting future cash flows). For that, you would need a Discounted Payback Period calculator.
