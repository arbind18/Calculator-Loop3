Stock Profit Calculator Guide

Our Stock Profit Calculator is designed to help traders and investors quickly determine their potential profit or loss from stock market transactions. By inputting your buy price, sell price, and number of shares, you can instantly see your Return on Investment (ROI) and net profit.

Why Use a Stock Calculator?

  • Accuracy: Eliminates manual calculation errors.
  • Speed: Get instant results for multiple scenarios.
  • Planning: Helps in setting target prices and stop-losses.
  • Fee Inclusion: Account for brokerage and other fees to see real returns.
How is ROI calculated?
ROI (Return on Investment) is calculated as: (Net Profit / Total Investment) * 100. It represents the percentage growth of your investment.
What is the Break-even Price?
The break-even price is the price at which you can sell your shares to cover your initial investment and all associated fees, resulting in zero profit and zero loss.
Does this calculator account for taxes?
You can include taxes in the "Brokerage/Fees" field. Simply add your estimated tax amount to your brokerage fees and enter the total sum.
