Understanding Interest Rate (CAGR)

The Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) is a useful measure of growth over multiple time periods. It can be thought of as the growth rate that gets you from the initial investment value to the ending investment value if you assume that the investment has been compounding over the time period.

Formula: CAGR = (Ending Value / Beginning Value)^(1 / Number of Years) - 1

This calculator helps you determine the effective annual interest rate required to grow your principal amount to the final amount over the specified time period.
