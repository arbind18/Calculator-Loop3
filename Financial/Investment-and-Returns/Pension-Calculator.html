About Pension Calculator

Planning for retirement is crucial to ensure financial independence. This Pension Calculator helps you estimate the corpus required to maintain your desired lifestyle post-retirement.

Why do I need a pension plan?
A pension plan ensures a steady stream of income after you stop working. It helps cover daily expenses, medical costs, and combat inflation during your retirement years.
How does inflation affect my pension?
Inflation reduces the purchasing power of money over time. A monthly expense of ?50,000 today will cost significantly more in 20-30 years. This calculator accounts for inflation to give you a realistic corpus estimate.
What is a safe withdrawal rate?
A safe withdrawal rate is the percentage of your corpus you can withdraw annually without running out of money. A common rule of thumb is 4%, but it varies based on market conditions and life expectancy.
