About Dividend Investing

Dividend investing is a strategy of buying stocks that pay regular dividends. This calculator helps you estimate your potential returns from such investments. By reinvesting dividends (DRIP), you can take advantage of compound growth, significantly increasing your portfolio value over time.

Dividend Yield: This is the ratio of a company's annual dividend compared to its share price. A higher yield means more income for every dollar invested.

Dividend Growth: Companies that regularly increase their dividends can provide a growing stream of passive income that helps beat inflation.
