Complete Guide to SIP Calculator 2025

A SIP Calculator is an online financial tool that helps you calculate the estimated returns on your Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) investments in mutual funds. With our advanced SIP calculator, you can plan your wealth creation journey by understanding how much your monthly investments will grow over time with the power of compounding.

What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way of investing in mutual funds where you invest a fixed amount regularly - monthly, quarterly, or annually. Instead of investing a large lumpsum amount, SIP allows you to invest small amounts periodically, making it easier on your pocket and helping you build wealth systematically over time.

What is the minimum amount required to start a SIP?
Most mutual funds in India allow you to start a SIP with as low as ?500 per month. However, some funds may have a minimum of ?1,000 or ?5,000. There is no maximum limit - you can invest any amount based on your financial capacity.
Can I stop or pause my SIP anytime?
Yes, you have complete flexibility to stop, pause, or modify your SIP at any time. You can pause for up to 6 months or stop permanently without any penalty.
What is a good return rate to expect from SIP?
Historically, equity mutual funds in India have delivered 12-15% CAGR over long periods (10+ years). Debt funds typically give 6-8% returns. However, past performance doesn't guarantee future returns.
What is Step-up SIP?
Step-up SIP (or Top-up SIP) allows you to increase your SIP amount periodically - annually or semi-annually by a fixed percentage (typically 5-15%). This is highly recommended as your income grows over time.
