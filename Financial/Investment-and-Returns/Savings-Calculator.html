About Savings Growth

Saving money is the first step towards financial freedom. But just saving isn't enough; you need your money to grow. This calculator helps you understand the power of compound interest and how regular contributions can build significant wealth over time.

What is compound interest?
Compound interest is the interest on a loan or deposit calculated based on both the initial principal and the accumulated interest from previous periods. It's often called "interest on interest".
How often should I contribute?
The more frequently you contribute, the faster your savings will grow due to compounding. Monthly contributions are a great habit to build.
