About Retirement Planning

Retirement planning is the process of determining retirement income goals and the actions and decisions necessary to achieve those goals. It includes identifying sources of income, estimating expenses, implementing a savings program, and managing assets and risk.

How much corpus is enough for retirement?
A common rule is the 25x rule, which suggests you need 25 times your annual expenses. However, this varies based on inflation, lifestyle, and life expectancy. This calculator provides a more precise estimate based on your specific inputs.
Why is inflation important?
Inflation erodes the purchasing power of money. ?40,000 today might be equivalent to ?1,50,000 in 20 years. Ignoring inflation is the biggest mistake in retirement planning.
