About Room Size Calculation

Calculating room size is essential for home improvement projects like flooring, painting, and furniture planning.

Formulas Used

  • Floor Area: Length × Width
  • Perimeter: 2 × (Length + Width)
  • Wall Area: Perimeter × Height

Common Uses

  • Flooring: Use the Floor Area to determine how much carpet, tile, or hardwood you need. Add 10-15% for waste.
  • Baseboards: Use the Perimeter to determine the length of baseboards needed.
  • Painting: Use the Wall Area to estimate paint gallons. One gallon typically covers 350-400 square feet.
