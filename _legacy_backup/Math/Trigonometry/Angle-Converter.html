Conversion Formulas

Degrees to Radians: $rad = deg \times \frac{\pi}{180}$

Radians to Degrees: $deg = rad \times \frac{180}{\pi}$

Degrees to Gradians: $grad = deg \times \frac{10}{9}$

Gradians to Degrees: $deg = grad \times \frac{9}{10}$

Radians to Gradians: $grad = rad \times \frac{200}{\pi}$

Gradians to Radians: $rad = grad \times \frac{\pi}{200}$
