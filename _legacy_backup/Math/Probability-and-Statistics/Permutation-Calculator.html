Permutation Calculator

Calculate the number of ways to arrange items (Order matters)

-

Formula

$$P(n, r) = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$$

Where:

$n$ = Total number of items in the set

$r$ = Number of items to arrange

$!$ = Factorial (e.g., $5! = 5 \times 4 \times 3 \times 2 \times 1$)

Note: In permutations, the order of arrangement matters (e.g., AB is different from BA).
