How It Works

Addition & Subtraction

Matrices are added or subtracted element by element. Both matrices must have the same dimensions.

$$ C_{ij} = A_{ij} \pm B_{ij} $$

Multiplication

Matrix multiplication involves taking the dot product of rows from the first matrix with columns from the second matrix.

$$ C_{ij} = \sum_k A_{ik} B_{kj} $$
