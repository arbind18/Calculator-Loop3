About Proportions

A proportion is an equation stating that two ratios are equal.
Direct Proportion: As one variable increases, the other increases (e.g., speed and distance).
Inverse Proportion: As one variable increases, the other decreases (e.g., speed and time).

Scale Models

Scale factors are used in maps and models to represent real-world objects at a smaller or larger size. A scale of 1:100 means 1 unit on the model equals 100 units in reality.

Quick Tips

  • Ensure units are consistent.
  • For direct proportion, cross-multiply.
  • For inverse proportion, multiply across.
