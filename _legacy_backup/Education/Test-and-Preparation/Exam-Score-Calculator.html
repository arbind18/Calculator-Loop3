How to Use

1. Enter your current grade in the class.

2. Enter the grade you want to achieve (Target Grade).

3. Enter the weight of the final exam (percentage).

4. Click "Calculate Required Score" to see what you need.

Formula

The calculator uses the following formula to determine your required score:

Required = (Target - Current × (1 - Weight)) / Weight
