How to Use

1. Enter the assignment name (e.g., Homework, Midterm).

2. Enter the grade you received (percentage).

3. Enter the weight of the assignment (percentage).

4. Click "Add Assignment" to include more items.

5. Click "Calculate Grade" to see your weighted average.

How it Works

The weighted grade is calculated by multiplying each grade by its weight (as a decimal) and summing the results. The sum is then divided by the total weight of all assignments entered.

Formula:

Grade = Σ(Grade × Weight) / Σ(Weight)
