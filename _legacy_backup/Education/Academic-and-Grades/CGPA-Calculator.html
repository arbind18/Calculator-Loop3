What is CGPA?

CGPA stands for Cumulative Grade Point Average. It is an educational grading system used in schools and colleges to measure overall academic performance. It is calculated by taking the mean of the GPA (Grade Point Average) that a student is awarded each semester, and is divided by the total number of credits.
