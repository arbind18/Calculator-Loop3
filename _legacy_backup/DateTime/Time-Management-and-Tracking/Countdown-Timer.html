FAQs

Q1: How do I set a countdown timer?
A: Enter your event name, select the target date and time, then click "Start Countdown". The timer will display days, hours, minutes, and seconds remaining.
Q2: Does the countdown update in real-time?
A: Yes! The countdown updates every second automatically, showing precise time remaining until your event.
Q3: Can I pause and resume the countdown?
A: Yes, use the Pause button to temporarily stop the countdown. Click Resume to continue from where you left off.
Q4: What happens when the countdown reaches zero?
A: When the timer reaches zero, all values will show "0" indicating your event time has arrived!
Q5: Can I set multiple countdowns?
A: Currently, you can track one countdown at a time. Use Reset to clear and start a new countdown.
Q6: Does it work offline?
A: Once loaded, the countdown works offline in your browser without internet connection.
Q7: Is this countdown timer free?
A: Yes! 100% free with unlimited use, no registration or subscription required.
Q8: Can I share my countdown?
A: You can share your screen or take screenshots of the countdown. Future updates may include direct sharing features.
Q9: What's the progress bar for?
A: The progress bar shows how much time has elapsed from when you started the countdown to your target date.
Q10: Does it work on mobile devices?
A: Absolutely! The countdown timer is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets.
