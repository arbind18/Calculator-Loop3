The Complete Guide to Zodiac Signs & Astrology

For thousands of years, humans have looked to the stars for guidance. Astrology is the study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs and the natural world. Our Zodiac Sign Calculator is your gateway to this ancient wisdom, providing instant access to your Western Sun Sign, Chinese Zodiac Animal, and Birthstone.

What is Astrology?

At its core, astrology is a symbolic language. It suggests that the position of the sun, moon, and planets at the exact moment of your birth creates a unique blueprint of your personality, potential, and life path. While often dismissed as pseudoscience by skeptics, millions find value in its archetypal insights for self-reflection and understanding interpersonal dynamics.

The 12 Zodiac Signs Explained

The Western Zodiac is divided into 12 signs, each occupying 30 degrees of celestial longitude. These signs are categorized by Elements (Fire, Earth, Air, Water) and Modalities (Cardinal, Fixed, Mutable).

Fire Signs: The Spark of Life

  • Aries (March 21 - April 19): The pioneer. Bold, ambitious, and impulsive. Aries loves to be number one.
  • Leo (July 23 - August 22): The king. Dramatic, creative, and self-confident. Leos love the spotlight.
  • Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21): The explorer. Optimistic, freedom-loving, and philosophical. They seek truth.

Earth Signs: The Builders

  • Taurus (April 20 - May 20): The stabilizer. Reliable, patient, and devoted. They value comfort and luxury.
  • Virgo (August 23 - September 22): The analyst. Loyal, analytical, and kind. They strive for perfection.
  • Capricorn (December 22 - January 19): The strategist. Disciplined, responsible, and self-controlled. They climb the mountain of success.

Air Signs: The Communicators

  • Gemini (May 21 - June 20): The messenger. Gentle, affectionate, and curious. They are the social butterflies.
  • Libra (September 23 - October 22): The diplomat. Cooperative, diplomatic, and gracious. They seek balance and harmony.
  • Aquarius (January 20 - February 18): The visionary. Progressive, original, and independent. They are the humanitarians.

Water Signs: The Feelers

  • Cancer (June 21 - July 22): The nurturer. Tenacious, highly imaginative, and loyal. They value home and family.
  • Scorpio (October 23 - November 21): The alchemist. Resourceful, brave, and passionate. They are intense and transformative.
  • Pisces (February 19 - March 20): The dreamer. Compassionate, artistic, and intuitive. They are the mystics of the zodiac.

The Chinese Zodiac (Shengxiao)

While Western astrology is based on months, Chinese astrology is based on a 12-year lunar cycle. Each year is represented by an animal. The legend goes that the Jade Emperor held a race to decide the order of the animals in the calendar.

  • Rat: Quick-witted and resourceful. (Years: 2020, 2008, 1996)
  • Ox: Diligent and dependable. (Years: 2021, 2009, 1997)
  • Tiger: Brave and confident. (Years: 2022, 2010, 1998)
  • Rabbit: Quiet and elegant. (Years: 2023, 2011, 1999)
  • Dragon: Confident and intelligent. (Years: 2024, 2012, 2000)
  • Snake: Enigmatic and wise. (Years: 2025, 2013, 2001)
  • Horse: Animated and active. (Years: 2026, 2014, 2002)
  • Goat: Calm and sympathetic. (Years: 2027, 2015, 2003)
  • Monkey: Sharp and smart. (Years: 2028, 2016, 2004)
  • Rooster: Observant and hardworking. (Years: 2029, 2017, 2005)
  • Dog: Lovely and honest. (Years: 2030, 2018, 2006)
  • Pig: Compassionate and generous. (Years: 2031, 2019, 2007)

Birthstones: Gems of Power

Birthstones are gems associated with a birth month, each having historical significance and healing powers. For example, Diamond (April) represents invincibility, while Ruby (July) represents passion and protection. Wearing your birthstone is thought to bring good luck and health.
