How to Use

Step 1: Choose Operation

Select whether you want to "Add Business Days" (for future dates) or "Subtract Business Days" (for past dates).

Step 2: Enter Details

Select your start date and enter the number of business days to calculate.

Step 3: Select Weekend Type

Choose which days count as weekends. The standard is Saturday & Sunday, but you can change this if needed.

Step 4: Calculate

Click the button to see the resulting date, along with a breakdown of calendar days and weekends skipped.

FAQs

Q1: Does this include public holidays?
A: This calculator focuses on weekends. For calculations involving specific public holidays, please use our "Working Days Calculator" which includes a holiday database.
Q2: How are weekends handled?
A: The calculator skips the selected weekend days (e.g., Sat/Sun) when counting. If you add 5 business days starting Monday, the result will be the following Monday (skipping Sat/Sun).
