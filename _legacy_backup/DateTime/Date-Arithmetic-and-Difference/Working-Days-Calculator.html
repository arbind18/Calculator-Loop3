How to Use the Working Days Calculator

Step 1: Enter Start Date

Select the day, month, and year for your starting date. This is typically the project start date or the beginning of your calculation period.

Step 2: Enter End Date

Select the day, month, and year for your ending date. This could be your project deadline or the end of your calculation period.

Step 3: Configure Options

Exclude Weekends: By default, Saturdays and Sundays are excluded. Uncheck if you need to include weekends.

Exclude Public Holidays: Check this to exclude all major Indian public holidays from 2025.

Step 4: Calculate

Click the "Calculate Working Days" button to see your results instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I calculate working days between two dates?
A: Enter your start and end dates, check the options to exclude weekends and/or holidays, then click Calculate. The result will show total working days with a detailed breakdown.
Q2: Does it automatically exclude weekends?
A: Yes! By default, the calculator excludes Saturdays and Sundays. You can toggle this option if your business operates on weekends.
Q3: Which public holidays are included?
A: We include all major Indian public holidays for 2025 including Republic Day (Jan 26), Independence Day (Aug 15), Gandhi Jayanti (Oct 2), Diwali, Holi, Eid, Christmas, and more.
Q4: Can I use this for payroll calculations?
A: Absolutely! This calculator is perfect for payroll. It accurately counts working days which is essential for calculating salaries, overtime, and leave balances.
Q5: What if my company has different holidays?
A: You can uncheck the "Exclude Public Holidays" option and manually subtract your company-specific holidays from the result.
Q6: How accurate is the calculation?
A: Our calculator is 100% accurate. It uses precise date algorithms to count each day individually and correctly identifies weekends and holidays.
Q7: Can I calculate working days across years?
A: Yes! You can calculate working days between any two dates, even if they span multiple years. The calculator handles year boundaries automatically.
Q8: Is this calculator free to use?
A: Yes! Our Working Days Calculator is 100% free with unlimited calculations. No registration or payment required.
Q9: Does it work on mobile phones?
A: Yes! The calculator is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and computers.
Q10: What's the difference between total days and working days?
A: Total days is the complete span between start and end dates. Working days exclude weekends and holidays based on your selected options.
