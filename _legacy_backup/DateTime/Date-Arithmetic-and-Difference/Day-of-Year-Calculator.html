FAQs

Q1: What is day of year?
A: Day of year (ordinal date) is the sequential day number from 1-365 (or 366 in leap years). January 1 is day 1, December 31 is day 365.
Q2: How do I find today's day number?
A: Click the "Today" button for instant results showing which day of the year today is.
Q3: What about leap years?
A: The calculator automatically detects leap years and adjusts to 366 days. February 29 is day 60 in leap years.
Q4: Can I use this for multiple years?
A: Yes! Works for any year from 1900 to 2100 with automatic leap year detection.
Q5: Is this calculator free?
A: Absolutely! 100% free with unlimited calculations, no registration required.
