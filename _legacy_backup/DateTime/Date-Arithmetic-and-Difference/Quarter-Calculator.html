FAQs

Q1: What are the 4 quarters?
A: Q1 = January-March, Q2 = April-June, Q3 = July-September, Q4 = October-December. Each quarter is 3 months.
Q2: How do I find which quarter today is?
A: Click the "Today" button to instantly see which quarter (Q1-Q4) today's date falls in.
Q3: What is fiscal quarter?
A: Fiscal quarters are 3-month periods used by businesses for financial reporting, planning, and performance tracking. Most companies use calendar quarters (Q1-Q4).
Q4: Do all companies use the same quarters?
A: Most use calendar quarters, but some companies have fiscal years that start in different months, shifting their quarter dates accordingly.
Q5: How many days in each quarter?
A: Q1 typically has 90 days, Q2 has 91, Q3 has 92, and Q4 has 92 (varies slightly in leap years).
Q6: Can I use this for multiple years?
A: Yes! Works for any year from 1900 to 2100 with accurate quarter calculations.
Q7: What is quarter progress?
A: Quarter progress shows how much of the current quarter has passed as a percentage, helping track quarterly goals.
Q8: Is this calculator free?
A: Absolutely! 100% free with unlimited calculations, no registration or subscription required.
Q9: Does it work offline?
A: Yes! Once loaded, all calculations happen in your browser without internet connection.
Q10: Why use quarters for business?
A: Quarters provide manageable time periods for goal setting, performance reviews, financial reporting, and strategic planning. They're standard in business worldwide.
