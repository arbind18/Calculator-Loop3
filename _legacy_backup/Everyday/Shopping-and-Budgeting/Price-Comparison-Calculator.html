Why Compare Unit Prices?

Comparing unit prices (price per ounce, pound, liter, etc.) is the best way to find the true value of a product. Often, larger "bulk" packages seem cheaper but may actually have a higher unit price than smaller packages on sale.

This calculator helps you quickly determine which item gives you more for your money.
