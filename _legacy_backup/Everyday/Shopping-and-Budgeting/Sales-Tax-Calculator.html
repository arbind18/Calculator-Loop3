About Sales Tax

Sales tax is a consumption tax imposed by the government on the sale of goods and services. In the United States, sales tax is levied at the state and local levels, meaning rates can vary significantly depending on your location.

Common Tax Rates (Approximate)

  • California: 7.25% - 10.75%
  • New York: 4% - 8.875%
  • Texas: 6.25% - 8.25%
  • Florida: 6% - 8%
